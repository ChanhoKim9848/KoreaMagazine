City and Location


Seoul

Seoul is capital city in South Korea.

It is the fourth largest metropolitan economy in the world and is larger than London and Paris.
If you visit South Korea from London by Airplane you will be firstly arriving at Seoul-Incheon International Airport Terminal 2


Busan

Busan is second biggest city in South Korea the largest port city in the world.
The Population of citizens is approximately 3.4 million. Busan is a center for international conventions, hosting APEC in 2005. It is also a center for sports tournaments in Korea, having hosted the 2002 Asian Games and FIFA World Cup. It is home to the world's largest department store, the Shinsegae Centum City.
